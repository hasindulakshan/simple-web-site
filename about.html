Stamp collecting is a popular hobby around the world. Stamp collectors are also known as philatelists.
One can explore the history and culture of different countries through their postage stamps.

Collecting stamps can be a fun and fulfilling hobby for people of all ages. Many collectors begin by saving stamps from letters they receive or buying inexpensive stamps br from dealers or online marketplaces. As they learn more about stamp collecting they begin to focus on specific themes or countries or invest in rarer or more valuable stamps.

Stamp collectors often use specialized tools and resources to identify and organize their collections. For example, stamp albums provide a way to display and store stamps, while catalogs and online databases can help collectors learn more about individual stamps and their value.
